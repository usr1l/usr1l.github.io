About Me

Hi, I'm Tony, a software engineer with a strong passion for creating innovative and impactful solutions. Transitioning from a marketing background, I decided to dive into the world of software engineering to pursue my love for problem-solving and technology. Over the past 8 months, I have been meticulously learning the craft at App Academy, where I have acquired a wide range of programming languages and technologies, such as Python, JavaScript, SQL, HTML, CSS, React, Redux, Flask, Express, Sequelize, and SQLAlchemy. My time there was also marked by significant collaboration and pair programming opportunities, enabling me to hone my team-working skills alongside my technical abilities.

Outside of tech, I am an avid reader and enjoy exploring various subjects, from history to philosophy. This curiosity extends to my work in software engineering, fueling my desire to understand the broader context of the solutions I create. Ultimately, my goal is to combine my technical expertise with my diverse interests to create innovative products that have a meaningful and lasting impact on society.

Biscord

An immersive chat and communication platform, inspired by Discord, that facilitates real-time conversations between users across various channels. Equipped with voice and text functionalities, the clone enables seamless collaboration and socialization. Users can create and manage servers, join channels, and exchange direct messages with their contacts. Developed using React-Redux, WebSockets for real-time communication, and an Express backend, the Discord Clone embodies an engaging, feature-rich user experience.

Casual Ink

A visually stunning online marketplace for art enthusiasts, collectors, and creators. This platform showcases a diverse array of artworks, from paintings to sculptures, and offers users the ability to bid on or purchase their favorite pieces. Artists can create profiles, upload their creations, and connect with potential buyers. With a sleek, responsive design using React-Redux, Python-Flask, and CSS3, the Artsy Clone combines an elegant aesthetic with a powerful, user-centric functionality, and provides a space for the art community to thrive.
